A bit about me

I’m Justine, a visual designer based in Montréal/Tiohtiá:ke, Canada. After completing a B.Sc. in Environment at McGill University, I worked for various non-profits in the sustainability field during which time my interest for design germinated from seed to sprout. I just recently graduated with a BFA in Design from Concordia University.

As a designer, I strongly believe in shaping the world we collectively want to inhabit, using my creative eye to bring attention to ideas that initiate, grow and sustain a sense of care for all. In my off time, you can find me on my bike, volunteering at Santropol Roulant's rooftop garden, or at home dancing and singing along to the Backstreet Boys.
