Bisto Tôt ou Tard

Branding proposal for a cozy bistro in th heart of the Plateau Mont-Royal.

YEARS

2020

ROLES

Branding, Layout Design

Located on the corner of Parc Lafontaine, Bistro Tôt ou Tard offers a relaxed atmosphere from morning to night, offering brunch until 4 pm everyday. In the summer, the bistro opens up 2 terraces where customers can enjoy the view of the park.

Bistro Tôt ou Tard is one of my go-to spots for food: for brunch on the weekend with friends or for take-out on weeknight I don't have energy left over for cooking. It seems like there's always for something for everyone on the menu no matter what the time of day.

I wanted to give my thanks to this place by imagining a new logo that would tell their story a little better. Tôt meaning early and Tard meaning late in French, the sun and moon I believed were ideal icons for communicating the bistro's ability to serve day and night. Inspired by their red-lit signage on the outside of the building, I also developed a new typeface in the same colour in a more modern sans-serif.
