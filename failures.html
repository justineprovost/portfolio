Failures Book

Book design using a small sample of False Starts, a play by local playwright Louis Patrick Leroux. Collaborative work with Nathalie Banger.

YEARS

2018

ROLES

Research, Layout Design, Collage

The book design was inspired by the chaotic and disconnected nature of the story. Choosing a collage/Dada approach, we took inspiration from David Carson, a graphic designer known for experimental typography and for developing the grunge aesthetic as popularized by Ray Gun magazine in the 1990s.

Visual research on David Carson. Examples of works featured in The End of Print: The Graphic Design of David Carson Book (2000) and David Carson: 2nd Sight: Graphik Design After the End of Print (1997) by Lewis Blackwell.

The imagery were used to emphasize the most interesting and/or discongruent elements of the text in order to recreate the experience of viewing the piece performed.

My initial concepts, test prints and typographic explorations.

The collage and layout work was split between Nathalie and I, and included a mix of magazine paper clippings and digital images found on the web. After a few variations of the cover page, we settled on the Lobster Mother figure, representing the character described at the end of the extract. The book was later hand-bound using bright yellow string.
