Sustainability Action Fund

Branding and web design for a non-profit empowering student-led sustainability initiatives at Concordia University.

YEARS

2014-2018

ROLES

Branding, Web Design

Commercial produced for special screenings on campus and on our website. Animation by Marielle Dalpé.

The Sustainability Action Fund provides funding to projects that inspire and develop a culture of sustainability at Concordia University. It supports projects across 7 themes: food, community, social justice, energy, resources & technology, education, health & well-being and waste. As Design and Communications coordinator, I was tasked with developing branding guidelines to use towards print and web communication tools.

Using the new brand guidelines, I developed the organization's new Worpress website, www.safconcordia.ca. I also designed and animated icons for to serve as visual aids for students using the online application portal.

In the 4.5 years I held the Design & Communication Coordinator position, the amount of funding requested by projects of the Sustainability Action Fund increased by 41%. In 2017-2018 alone, an estimated 11,940 people had attended events funded by the organization.

While sustainability is at the heart of the organization's image, it was important for the visuals to communicate the Sustainability Action Fund's approachability as well as its trustworthiness. Committed to the growth and long-term development of sustainability and off campus for over 10 years, it was time for the brand to reflect how this non-profit has made itself a name in Concordia University and in the wider community of Montreal.
